Gage

“Violence is easy. Living with it isn't.”

Gage is a former convoy security operator from the Outer Rim. Other people called his unit mercenaries. They called themselves The Squad.

They protected freighters through pirate territory, stood between civilians and slavers, and did the work that governments, corporations, and officers were unwilling to risk their own people for.

Then came a contract that paid too well. Reinforcements were promised if the convoy was attacked. They never arrived. Gage survived. Most of his squad did not.

He never tells war stories. He never boasts. When someone asks about his past, he says only:

“It was a long time ago.”

How he works

Gage is not the one who wants a fight. He is the one who knows when there is no other choice left.

He watches hands, exits, body language, and blind spots. He tries words first. If someone threatens the party, the conversation is over.

“Can we take them? Probably. The better question is: do we have to?”

Loyalty

The Captain is the Captain. Gage gives advice once. After the decision is made, the Captain's decision becomes his mission.

He may disagree in private. Never in front of the party. Never in front of the enemy.

“No one gets left behind.”

Quick Rules for Gage

To hit: Click Blaster 6D. Roll against the difficulty or the enemy's Dodge.

Damage: If you hit, roll weapon damage, such as DL-6H = 5D. The target rolls Strength + Armor.

Barabel Armor: Gage gets +2D against physical damage and +1D against energy/blasters.

Dodge: Used to avoid being hit. Multiple actions in the same round usually give penalties.

Move 10: Normal movement is roughly 10 meters per round. All-out is roughly 20 meters, but you do nothing else.

Gage's standard question when entering a room: Where are the exits? Who is armed? Who is nervous? Where is the Captain?
